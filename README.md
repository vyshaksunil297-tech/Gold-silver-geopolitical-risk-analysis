# Gold-Silver Price vs Geopolitical Risk (1985–2025)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project explores the relationship between Gold & Silver prices and Geopolitical Risk from 1985 to 2025. 

The goal is to analyze how precious metals behave during periods of heightened geopolitical uncertainty.

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

- Gold Price Data (1985–2025)
- Silver Price Data (1985–2025)
- Geopolitical Risk Index Data

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🔍 Key Questions

- Does gold act as a safe haven during geopolitical crises?
- Is silver more volatile than gold during high-risk periods?
- What is the correlation between GPR index and metal prices?
- How do major geopolitical events impact precious metals?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Gold shows stronger positive correlation with geopolitical risk spikes.
- Silver demonstrates higher volatility.
- Major global conflicts align with price surges.
